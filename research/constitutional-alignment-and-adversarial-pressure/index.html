Constitutional Alignment Under Adversarial Pressure

By Nathan Heath · 2026-04-30 · AI Safety

Abstract

Constitutional AI demonstrated a scalable route to harmlessness using rule-guided self-critique and AI feedback. However, adversarial pressure reveals a persistent need for independent red-team protocols and post-deployment monitoring.

Alignment stack diagram

Argument

Constitutional alignment should be treated as an internal control mechanism, not a full security guarantee. High-stakes deployments still require adversarial testing regimes with measurable failure taxonomies.
