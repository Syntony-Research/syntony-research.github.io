HELM-Style Evaluation for Executive Decision Contexts

By Nathan Heath · 2026-04-25 · Evaluation Science

Abstract

Holistic Evaluation of Language Models (HELM) demonstrated why single-metric reporting obscures operational tradeoffs. Executive governance needs multi-metric portfolios that preserve this structure while mapping to decision thresholds.

Multi-metric visual

Implementation note

For governance decision cycles, each metric should map to escalation rules, acceptable ranges, and override conditions. This converts evaluation from reporting to control architecture.
