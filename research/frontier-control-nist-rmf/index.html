From Capability to Control: Applying NIST AI RMF to Frontier Deployments

By Nathan Heath · 2026-05-01 · Governance

Abstract

NIST AI RMF 1.0 established a voluntary lifecycle framework with four functions, Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage, for trustworthy AI risk management. This article adapts that architecture for frontier-model operations where capability updates outpace governance refresh cycles.

Model

Operationally, frontier programs should treat the Govern function as a continuously updated control plane, not an annual policy artifact.

Key claims

  • Governance lag is itself a measurable risk variable.
  • Mapping must include sociotechnical misuse pathways, not only model error classes.
  • Measurement portfolios should combine model-level, system-level, and human-process indicators.
