Privacy Policy

Last updated May 10, 2026. This policy summarizes how Syntony handles personal information submitted through this website and during business inquiries.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, including name, organization, email address, engagement type, scheduling details, inquiry details, and message content. We may also receive ordinary technical information such as IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, evaluate potential engagements, provide services, maintain business records, and improve website performance. We do not sell personal information.

Contact form and scheduling data

Contact forms are configured for a static-site form provider and scheduling is handled through Cal.com. Information submitted through those services is processed according to their respective terms and privacy practices.

Analytics and cookies

The site may use privacy-respecting analytics, such as Plausible, to understand aggregate site usage without cross-site tracking. We do not use advertising cookies. Embedded third-party services may set their own cookies or storage when used.

Confidentiality and client materials

Service-related confidential information is handled under the applicable NDA, statement of work, or client agreement. Do not submit highly sensitive information through the public contact form before an appropriate confidentiality arrangement is in place.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@syntonyresearch.org.
