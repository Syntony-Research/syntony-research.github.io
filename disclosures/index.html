Disclosures

This page summarizes affiliations, conflict practices, and independence commitments for Syntony Research.

Independence

Syntony Research provides independent research and advisory services. We aim to separate evidence, inference, uncertainty, and recommendation in client work and public materials.

Conflicts of interest

Potential conflicts are reviewed at intake and disclosed when required by law, contract, professional obligation, or sound client practice. Syntony Research may decline work where independence or confidentiality cannot be maintained.

Affiliations

Nathan Heath’s affiliations and networks include Cloud Security Alliance, Rutgers AI Ethics Lab, Truman National Security Project, BlueDot Impact, NextGen, Hertog Foundation, GLG, and CSET Georgetown connection through Duco. These affiliations do not imply endorsement of Syntony Research by those organizations unless expressly stated.

Client confidentiality

Client identities, materials, and engagement details are confidential unless disclosure is authorized. Public references to engagement types are anonymized or generalized.

Regulatory status

Syntony Research is not a law firm, accounting firm, broker-dealer, investment adviser, or government agency. Services do not constitute legal, financial, or regulatory advice.
