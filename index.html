syn·to·ny

Coordination in phase.

Our name reflects a practical thesis: technical systems and institutions perform best when they are tuned to one another.

The work is not merely to make models more capable or policies more polished. The work is to align evidence, controls, incentives, and escalation paths before capability outruns accountability.
