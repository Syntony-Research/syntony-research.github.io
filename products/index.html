Product posture

Decision support, not a stamp.

The suite is being designed for client-specific AI risk ecosystems. It will help teams make evidence-backed decisions, preserve review trails, and understand where risk is amplifying or dampening.

It is not an assurance seal, certification mark, launch approval, insurance score, or substitute for legal advice.
