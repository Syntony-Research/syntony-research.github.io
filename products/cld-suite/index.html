01Platform thesis

Why causal mapping matters for AI risk.

The market has platforms for AI model inventories, control tracking, and audit workflows. What's missing is upstream: understanding how capability pressure, evaluation gaps, deployment incentives, regulation, and alignment risk reinforce each other.

AI-risk specific

Prebuilt systems focus on frontier AI, agentic deployment, red-team evidence, governance lag, alignment dynamics, compute, insurance, cyber, public trust, and institutional oversight.

Client-ecosystem specific

Paid licenses adapt the map to the client’s products, deployment surfaces, review cadence, loss pathways, ownership model, control environment, and external constraints.

Decision-facing

Each map is designed for briefings and workshops where the decision is concrete: release, hold, monitor, insure, escalate, regulate, invest, or redesign.

04Product boundary

Scope and boundaries.

The CLD Suite focuses on causal risk structure, not model inventory or enterprise GRC. It explains how risks interact before and after controls are chosen.

Independent method base

Built on public causal loop diagram methodology, systems dynamics, and applied AI risk research.

Not model inventory GRC

Governance platforms track models, controls, and audit evidence. The CLD Suite maps the dynamics that make those controls necessary.

Not a generic diagram tool

Ships with domain-specific AI risk systems, guided narratives, and deployment patterns for briefings, workshops, and dashboards.

Method grounding: causal loop diagrams from systems dynamics literature, applied to AI risk following recent work on hazard-to-harm causal mapping.
