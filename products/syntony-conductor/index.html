Positioning

Red teaming tells you what happened. Syntony Conductor helps decide what follows.

The product is built around the practical handoff from technical evidence to governance action: what failed, why it matters, who owns it, what control changes, and what decision record should exist.

01Product suite

Built around the evidence-to-decision workflow.

Syntony Conductor is planned as a modular SaaS suite. The first release will focus on evidence registration, finding classification, and decision-record generation.

Casebook

Central evidence register for red-team reports, transcripts, tool traces, screenshots, reproduction notes, eval bundles, and monitor alerts.

Findings

Structured conversion of raw evidence into failure modes, governance findings, severity, confidence, reproducibility, and required decisions.

AlignMap

Mapping layer for reward hacking, goal hijacking, oversight failure, tool misuse, autonomy escalation, policy evasion, and benchmark gaming.

ControlMap

Translate findings into owners, escalation triggers, monitoring requirements, rollback criteria, and framework-aligned control actions.

LiveMonitor

Detect governance-relevant drift from production AI behavior, trace anomalies, user reports, tool calls, and eval regressions.

Decision Room

Workflow for ship, hold, mitigate, monitor, and residual-risk acceptance decisions with exportable records.
