Product direction

Evidence translation, not assurance certification.

The product line is designed to help teams understand what red-team results imply for governance, alignment, release decisions, and monitoring. It does not issue seals, safety stamps, certifications, or procurement approvals.

Boundary

What this product line will not claim.

  • No certification. Syntony software will not award safety seals, badges, or approval marks.
  • No assurance stamp. Outputs are decision support, not a claim that an AI system is safe.
  • No insurance score. Findings can inform risk conversations, but they are not underwriting products.
  • No one-click compliance. Framework mappings are evidence organization aids, not legal determinations.
