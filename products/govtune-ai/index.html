01What it is

What GovTune AI does.

GovTune AI is focused on a narrow problem: helping organizations move from AI risk evidence to governance-ready interpretation. The public page describes the outcome, not the underlying product architecture.

Evidence interpretation

Helps translate AI risk signals into language that governance owners can review.

Decision support

Frames the questions, assumptions, and uncertainty around a pending AI risk decision.

Client tailoring

Initial deployments will be configured around each client's systems, evidence sources, and decision environment.

02Why it matters

The problem: evidence that never reaches a decision.

Red-team reports, evaluation notes, and monitoring alerts often reach different teams in different formats. Technical teams see behavior. Legal and policy teams see exposure. Executives see delay. GovTune AI is being designed to help those groups reason from the same evidence without pretending uncertainty has disappeared.

For evaluators

Preserve what the evidence can and cannot support.

For governance teams

Translate evidence into reviewable questions and records.

For leadership

See the decision posture without losing the technical caveats.

03Release path

Availability.

Initial deployments will be tailored for organizations with existing AI risk evidence and active governance questions. Broader access will follow after the product and IP posture are ready.

GovTune AI is intended for decision support and will be paired with client-specific advisory configuration.
