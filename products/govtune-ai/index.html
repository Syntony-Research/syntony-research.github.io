01What it is

What GovTune AI does.

GovTune AI is focused on a narrow problem: moving from AI risk evidence and cross-domain context to governance interpretation. The public page describes the outcome, not the underlying product architecture.

Evidence interpretation

Turns AI risk signals into language that governance owners can review.

Decision support

Frames the questions, assumptions, and uncertainty around a pending AI risk decision.

Context integration

Connects evaluation evidence with client-specific risk ecosystems, governance frameworks, and external signals.

02Why it matters

The problem: evidence that never reaches a decision.

Red-team reports, evaluation notes, monitoring alerts, geopolitical shifts, and sector-specific risk signals often reach different teams in different formats. Technical teams see behavior. Legal and policy teams see exposure. Executives see delay. GovTune AI is being designed so those groups can reason from the same evidence without pretending uncertainty has disappeared.

For evaluators

Preserve what the evidence can and cannot support.

For governance teams

Translate evidence into reviewable questions and records.

For leadership

See the decision posture without losing the technical caveats.

03Release path

Availability.

Initial deployments will be tailored for organizations with existing AI risk evidence and active governance questions. Broader access will follow after the product and IP posture are ready.

GovTune AI is intended for decision support and will be paired with client-specific advisory configuration.
