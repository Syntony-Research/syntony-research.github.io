Biography

Nathan Heath is the founder of Syntony Research. His work sits at the intersection of AI safety evaluation, strategic risk, and governance implementation.

He has advised organizations navigating frontier-model risk, institutional oversight requirements, and cross-functional decision architecture.

His writing has appeared in War on the Rocks, World Politics Review, RAND Europe publications, and The Washington Post.
