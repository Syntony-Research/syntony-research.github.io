The syntony thesis

Syntony means coordinated tuning. For advanced AI, the coordination problem is sociotechnical: model capabilities, deployment pressures, monitoring, legal obligations, and institutional decision-making all interact.

AI safety is a pattern of evidence and control, not a single benchmark or compliance checkbox. It requires understanding what a system can do, how it can fail, who decides, and how quickly intervention can happen.

We work where technical evaluation becomes institutional action: adversarial testing that informs controls, governance design grounded in observed system behavior, and research that decision-makers can act on.
