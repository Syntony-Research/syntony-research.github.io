When to engage us

Pre-deployment assurance

You are releasing a new model or capability and need independent adversarial evaluation before deployment.

Regulatory preparation

A regulator, board, or standards body requires documented adversarial testing as part of a conformity or risk assessment.

Post-incident investigation

An incident or near-miss has revealed potential failure modes that your internal testing did not catch.

Independent validation

You need a third party to verify internal safety claims before communicating them to stakeholders or the public.
